About Me

Hello, and welcome! I’m Hagen Paul Pfeifer, Chief Software Strategist based in Munich. My focus spans enterprise-wide technology strategy, engineering governance, and the standardisation of processes and development practices. I specialise in the architectural design and strategic oversight of complex software systems, building robust digital solutions that not only work reliably but also drive innovation—always under real-world constraints of economics, maintainability, security, and sustainability.

At the core of my expertise is a deep understanding of software, with particular strengths in operating systems like the Linux kernel, software analysis, security, embedded systems, and network protocols. These aren’t just specialties—they’re the pillars I use to design scalable, efficient systems.

I’ve contributed to the shaping of TCP, IPv6, and BGPv4 standards, helping to evolve the modern internet. My upstream Linux kernel work spans several subsystems, including the network stack, tracing, perf, epoll, and the KVM hypervisor.

Beyond the purely technical, I care about sharing knowledge: I write for heise, deliver talks, organise conferences, and participate in professional communities such as ACM and the German Informatics Society (Gesellschaft für Informatik e. V.).

Whether you are exploring cutting-edge software solutions or simply interested in the ever-evolving world of technology, I hope you find this space insightful. Welcome to my corner of the digital universe.
